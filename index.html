As you probably have experienced, most pages retrieved by the search enginees are not interesting for the topic you are researching. You spend vast amount of time opening an skimming pages. This shows that Pagerank is too general. It does not take your specific need and interest into account. And, day after day you have to type in and refine search words again, and again...

telescope.no automates the search and stores interesting links in folders. It lets you rate pages and thereby gradually build your interest profile. Telescope.no does not sleep even if you forget about your research topic for a while. It lets you log in or send you emails.

You log in and read news, articles, commentary, and books whenever you have time. It does not replace search. Rather is complements it by adding a second layer (filter). And, it is searching more broadly, going further than scores like Pagerank. The goal is to find new, relevant information in less time and using less energy.

telescope.no stores links to pages (documents) in folders which you can organize. This let you go back to previous highly rated pages and fasilitates your own repetition and learning. You can also write notes.

A free version keeps your profile and stored pages (documents) public. The number of keywords (features) are limited in the free version.
